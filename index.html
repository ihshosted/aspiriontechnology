Who are we?

Aspirion Technology is a boutique Computer Support and Network Integration Firm.

Aspirion Technology provides high quality, affordable IT Support and IT Professional Services for small to mid-size businesses throughout the US.

Managing and supporting the day-to-day IT infrastructure of an organization, while critical to the smooth running of the business, is highly demanding on small businesses with no IT support staff or for companies with limited IT resources. The solution for many of our clients is - IT Managed Services.

Under the IT Managed Services plan, we manage everything related to your desktops, network devices and servers. Aspirion Technology does specialized SQL and Oracle database monitoring, e-mail protection and management, network management, server maintenance, desktop support, performance monitoring, patching, anti-virus updates, software deployment, VOIP setup and maintenance and much more.

Remote Infrastructure Management

Aspirion Technology’s Managed Services Remote Infrastructure Management (RIM) provides 24x7 support for your critical and non-critical systems using a proactive approach to manage client applications and deliver network notification as soon as a critical application or server is down. Remotely managed system administration enables the customer to concentrate on their core business without the worry of managing their technical department, allowing customers to reduce costs and improve response times to daily tasks and critical issues.

Aspirion Technology’s Managed Services Remote Infrastructure Management monitors and tracks key network parameters such as network latency and packet loss – identifying the source of congestions in the Internet or private network.

Highlights:

  • 85% first call resolution

  • Proactive management processes
